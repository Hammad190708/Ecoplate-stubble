A Green Revolution Against Stubble Burning

Our Vision

We envision a world where agricultural waste no longer pollutes our skies. By converting stubble into sustainable and biodegradable products, we protect our environment while empowering farmers.

Why EcoPlates?

  • Combats stubble burning and its harmful effects
  • Reduces air pollution and promotes cleaner cities
  • Empowers farmers with an alternate income
  • Supports circular economy through eco-manufacturing
  • Affordable, biodegradable, and compostable tableware

Our Products

  1. Eco Plates: 100% biodegradable, microwave-safe, and leak-proof.
  2. Takeaway Boxes: Eco-friendly packaging for food businesses.
  3. Compostable Wraps: Perfect for daily household and market use.

How It Works

  1. Farmers provide agricultural stubble after harvest.
  2. Stubble is collected and processed into raw pulp.
  3. Eco-friendly plates and packaging are produced.
  4. Distributed to local businesses and consumers.

Environmental Impact

Every ton of stubble reused through our process prevents nearly 1.5 tons of carbon emissions. Our mission aims to save over 50,000 tons of stubble from being burnt each year.
